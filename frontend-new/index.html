About PurgeX

No NFT is worthless again — transform idle assets into earning potential

Our Vision

We believe every token has value. Dust tokens — those tiny leftovers from swaps, airdrops, and failed transactions — represent billions in trapped capital. PurgeX unlocks that value by automatically consolidating dust into a productive asset: PRGX.

But we go further: by staking PRGX, you earn continuous rewards, creating a sustainable income from what was once worthless.

The Problem

  • Wallets accumulate dozens of tiny token balances (dust)
  • Dust is uneconomical to sell individually (gas > value)
  • Users keep these dead tokens, cluttering their portfolio
  • Capital sits idle, unable to be put to work

The Solution

  • Auto-sweep: One-click conversion of all dust to PRGX
  • Staking rewards: Earn 6.4 PRGX per second for every staked token
  • Low fee: Only 1% on sweeps, much cheaper than manual claiming
  • Decentralized control: Multisig treasury, community governance
  • Secure: Verified contracts, locked liquidity, no admin keys
