Frequently Asked Questions

What is PurgeX?

PurgeX is a DeFi protocol on PulseChain that allows users to convert worthless ERC-20 dust tokens into valuable PRGX tokens. Users can then stake PRGX to earn rewards distributed at 6.4 PRGX per second.

How does the sweep work?

Connect your wallet, discover dust tokens, select which ones to sweep, approve them, and execute the sweep transaction. The protocol calculates the PRGX output based on token values and charges a 5% fee.

Is PurgeX safe?

Yes. All contracts are verified, audited by independent security firms, use multisig governance, have LP tokens locked for 2+ years, and contain no admin keys or special privileges.

What's the 5% fee for?

The 5% fee sustains the protocol, funds development, and contributes to the treasury. This fee is permanently capped and cannot be changed by anyone.

Can I unstake anytime?

Yes. There are no lock-up periods for staking. You can unstake your PRGX tokens at any time and receive both your principal and any accumulated rewards.
