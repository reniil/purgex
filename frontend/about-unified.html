About PurgeX

Transforming idle assets into value, one token at a time

Our Vision

"No token is worthless again"
— PurgeX Mission

We believe every token and NFT in your wallet should have value. PurgeX transforms scattered dust tokens into valuable PRGX, creating a unified ecosystem where idle assets generate returns through staking and AI-governed treasury management.

The Challenge & Solution

🔍 The Problem

  • Users have small value tokens scattered across wallets
  • Small amounts are unusable and costly to move individually
  • Wasted capital in idle or forgotten projects
  • No easy way to consolidate scattered token amounts

💡 The Solution

  • One-click bulk token conversion to PRGX
  • Transparent 5% fee structure
  • Stake PRGX to earn more PRGX (6.4/sec)
  • AI-governed treasury and ecosystem
