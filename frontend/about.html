About PurgeX

Transforming idle assets into value, one dust token at a time

Our Vision

"No NFT is worthless again"
— PurgeX Mission

We believe every token in your wallet should have value. PurgeX transforms scattered dust tokens into valuable PRGX, creating a unified ecosystem where idle assets generate returns through staking and governance.

🔍 The Problem

  • Users have dust tokens (<$1) scattered across wallets
  • Dust is unusable and costly to move individually
  • Wasted capital in dead or abandoned projects
  • No easy way to consolidate small token amounts

💡 The Solution

  • One-click bulk dust sweeping to PRGX
  • Transparent 1% fee structure
  • Stake PRGX to earn more PRGX (6.4/sec)
  • Community-governed treasury and ecosystem
