### **4. FAQ Document**

**Q: What is PurgeX?**  
A: A dust sweep protocol on PulseChain that converts worthless tokens into PRGX.

**Q: How much does it cost?**  
A: Only 5% of the swept token value as a fee. 50% of fees are burned, 30% goes to treasury, 20% to staking rewards.

**Q: What is the 100 PRGX bonus?**  
A: Every token you sweep earns you an additional 100 PRGX, regardless of balance. Even zero-balance tokens give the bonus! This gamifies wallet cleaning.

**Q: Is my liquidity safe?**  
A: Yes! 500M PRGX is locked in WPLS pool for 2 years via Team Finance. All LP tokens are locked.

**Q: What is staking?**  
A: Stake PRGX to earn passive income. Tiered APRs: Bronze 10%, Silver 12%, Gold 15%, Diamond 20%. Sweep bonuses get multipliers!

**Q: How do I get PRGX?**  
A: Sweep tokens → Earn PRGX. Or buy on PulseX (PRGX/WPLS pool). Or stake to earn more.

**Q: What is the total supply?**  
A: 1,000,000,000 PRGX (1 billion). Current circulating: depends on unlocks.

**Q: Is the contract audited?**  
A: Not yet. We're in early stages. Use at your own risk. Audits planned after Phase 3.

**Q: How do I qualify for bonuses?**  
A: Simply sweep any token (even with zero balance). You'll automatically receive 100 PRGX bonus per token swept.

**Q: What is the referral program?**  
A: Share your link. Referrer gets 5% of referee's sweep earnings (in PRGX). Referee gets 50 PRGX bonus on first sweep.
